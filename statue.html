This project delves deeply into the artistic and photographic technique of chiaroscuro, focusing on the interplay of light and shadow to create a sense of depth and intensity. Through a careful study of textures, I explore how light interacts with different surfaces and materials, revealing intricate details that might otherwise go unnoticed. The work transitions from close-up studies, where individual textures and forms are isolated to emphasize

their unique qualities, to full-figure compositions that embody the dramatic effects of chiaroscuro. By shifting from micro to macro perspectives, this project aims to showcase the impact of light and shadow on perception, creating powerful contrasts that bring each subject to life with a sense of presence and dimension.
