This project explores a conceptual color system designed to enhance the packaging of a chewing gum line, with Stimorol as the chosen case study. The concept revolves around fruit as the central theme, visually linking the gum flavors to colors inspired by natural ingredients. The goal is to create a fresh and engaging identity that highlights the connection between taste and visual perception.

The design approach focuses on three dimensionality, color relationships, and dynamic pairings, resulting in packaging that conveys energy and vibrancy.
