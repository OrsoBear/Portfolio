This is the project that relaxes me the most. Since childhood, I’ve always had a deep passion for cars, especially vintage ones, so much so that I now drive an old Volkswagen Beetle. Over the years, however, I’ve developed a strange fascination with vintage and quirky vans; I’ve always found them funny and charming. So, I started drawing them in a cartoon-like style, taking inspiration from movies, television, the world around me, and brands I love.

Eventually, I decided to gather all my drawings on a social media page, which I update sporadically and without pressure, keeping this passion as something purely fun and enjoyable.
