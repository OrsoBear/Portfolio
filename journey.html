This project explores surreal, atmospheric photography inspired by the idea of an otherworldly journey. Set against the dramatic backdrop of the Gotthard Pass, I transform this striking location into an alien landscape through vibrant, exaggerated colors and heavy contrasts. The Passâ€™s rugged, mysterious qualities are amplified, creating an atmosphere that feels distant and dreamlike evoking a world that seems far removed from Earth.

By intensifying shadows and casting the landscape in dark, almost ominous hues, I aim to evoke the eerie beauty and isolation of a journey to Mars. Each photograph invites viewers to step into this imagined world, offering a glimpse of what it might be like to wander through a place both hauntingly beautiful and unsettlingly foreign.
