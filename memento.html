For several years, even before I started shooting on film, I explored instant photography using the Instax Mini format. Over time, I collected hundreds of shots, and to give them new meaning, I decided to create an Instagram page. Here, I use a three-grid layout to build visual triptychs, three posts a week, three images linked by color, shape, or content.

I'm deeply connected to instant photography for its inherent here and now essence. Each shot captures a fleeting moment, permanently imprinted on a 54 x 86 mm card. This project is a way to tell stories through visual fragments, celebrating the aesthetics and uniqueness of instant photography.
