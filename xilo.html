This brand identity project is dedicated to XILO, a fictional company whose name comes from the Greek ξύλο, meaning "wood" or "made of wood." The brand draws inspiration from Swiss design, known for its simplicity, functionality, and an aesthetic rooted in geometry and form reduction. The project's artisanal soul is reflected in the handcrafted wooden furniture, carefully produced by a young and creative team of designers and specialized carpenters.

The products stand out for their predominant use of light birch wood and are divided into several categories: tables, chairs, bookshelves, chandeliers, and decorative objects. The brand’s visual identity is designed to convey the perfect balance between tradition and innovation, with a clean and refined aesthetic that celebrates the beauty of wood and the precision of craftsmanship.
