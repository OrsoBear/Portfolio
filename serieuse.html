This brand identity project is inspired by the analysis of Pina Bausch, the iconic German dancer and choreographer. Her work was deconstructed and reinterpreted to create a hypothetical cosmetics brand that reflects her values, aesthetics, and artistic philosophy. Through in-depth research, a concept was developed that merges movement, emotion, and authenticity into a unique visual identity.

From the naming—crafted through brainstorming—to the creation of the logo and font selection, every element was designed to convey the evocative mood of her work. The project is completed with a visual campaign where striking images interact with the brand’s identity, crafting an immersive experience that tells the story of beauty through the language of dance and artistic expression.
