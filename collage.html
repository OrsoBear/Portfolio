This project is a collection of experimental collages born from a spontaneous and instinctive creative process. Each composition comes to life through the selection of visual details gathered from extensive research on specialized sites, then assembled on high-resolution backgrounds. There is no rigid planning, each piece evolves over time, modified and refined until it reaches a visually satisfying balance that reflects my personal taste.

The style is deliberately exaggerated, almost trashy, with bold and unpredictable combinations. The project embraces a relaxed and free-spirited approach, without predefined rules, leaving room for the pure joy of experimentation. Each collage becomes a fragment of random thoughts transformed into images, a visual exploration fueled by chaos and creativity.
