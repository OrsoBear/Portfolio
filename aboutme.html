About me

I'm Nakai Borges, passionate about visual design and creativity in all its forms. After two years of studying for a Bachelor’s in Visual Communication at SUPSI in Mendrisio, I am now continuing with a professional Bachelor’s in Visual Design at SSS'aa in Lugano. My interest in design goes hand in hand with my love for creating: in my free time, I enjoy model-making, painting, digital drawing, and 3D printing, always finding new ways to express my creativity.

In recent years, I've developed a keen interest in photography, particularly analog and instant photography, experimenting with Instax, Polaroid, and a few vintage film cameras passed down from my grandfather.

This journey into traditional photography has taught me to see the world with a more attentive and patient eye.

Lately, I’ve also been exploring image generation through AI, a field that fascinates me for its endless creative possibilities. I have a strong interest in printed graphics, and I currently help manage a school magazine project involving students at the Centro Scolastico per le Industrie Artistiche in Lugano.

I believe design is a continuous journey of exploration, and I'm excited to see where this path will lead me.
